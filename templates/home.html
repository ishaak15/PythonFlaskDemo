About MagellanGo

MagellanGO is a single Web, iOS, and Android App for all your travel needs.

With MagellanGO, you can apply for an eVisa, create a trip, complete your travel documents, upload your health records, pay for your travel insurance, book ground transportation, select excursions in your destination and buy duty-free products. The App provides a step-by-step checklist for all your travel needs and a Government Approved QR Code to begin your journey.
